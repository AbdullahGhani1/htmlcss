The History Of Our Hotel

Hotel BT (Est. 1995): In 1995, recognizing the growing popularity of beach destinations, the Magnolia Hotel Chain expanded with the opening of Hotel BT. Nestled along a pristine coastline, the resort boasted breathtaking ocean views, pristine sandy beaches, and a range of recreational activities. It soon became synonymous with relaxation and rejuvenation, attracting visitors from far and wide.
